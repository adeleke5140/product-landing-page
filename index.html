The Boys

In a world where superheroes embrace the darker side of their massive celebrity and fame, THE BOYS centers on a group of vigilantes known informally as “The Boys,” who set out to take down corrupt superheroes with no more than their blue collar grit and a willingness to fight dirty.

Details

Starring: Karl Urban, Jack Quaid, Antony Starr Genres: Comedy, Action IMDB Rating: 8.7 Year: 2019 Rated: 18+

Reviews

The Boys is an absolutely bonkers superhero show in the best way. The Boys has a myriad of memorable, nuanced characters, that distinguish it from similar offerings within the genre.

The Boys takes you on an obscene, violent, and sometimes comedic journey that explores what happens when the superheroes you admire on camera, are actually jerks behind it.
